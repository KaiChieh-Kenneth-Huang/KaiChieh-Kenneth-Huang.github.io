Double Sweep

When making a sweeping stroke with a broom, the effective sweep length is shorter than the stroke length because the sweeping motion is only effective when the bristles of the broom are at certain angles to the floor. To increase the effective sweep length of each stroke, I propose the concept of a double-headed broom.

  • System Description

System Description

The proposed double-headed broom has two heads that can be turned and locked at various angles.

  • Next

When the heads are opened up, each sweeping stroke is made more effective because the second set of bristles will be within the effective angle to the floor when the first set can no longer effectively clean the floor.

  • Next

The heads can be closed up for easy storage or for use as a regular broom. Additionally, the bending heads allow the user to easily clean places that are difficult to clean with a traditional broom. These places include under a sofa and on top of a shelf.
