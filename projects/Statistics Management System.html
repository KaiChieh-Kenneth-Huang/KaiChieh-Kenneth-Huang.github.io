Statistics Management System

The South Africa Cape Town Mission of the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints originally collected performance statistics of missionaries by having them send in excel sheets weekly. The headquarter spent time each week to manually sort the collected data. Moreover, only a very small portion of the data was used as reference for organization management, largely due to the lack of readability of the raw data. The statistics management system developed automatically processes statistics missionaries send online and visualizes the data collected over several weeks. This system revolutionized the use of statistics in the organization and continues to be in use today.

System Description

The system, built using Google Apps Script upon Google Sheets, comprises 8 analogous programmed spreadsheets for the leaders of 8 mission zones and a programmed master spreadsheet for the headquarter. The zone leaders submit performance statistics and written reports of missionaries under their leadership weekly onto the system.

The system automatically generates graphs tracking selected performance statistics over a 12-week window. The master spreadsheet parses and organizes all information from each of the 8 spreadsheets and displays their performance graphs and written reports.

The graphs, statistics to enter, and the number of people in each zone are made customizable via the UI, in response to the constantly changing needs of the mission.
