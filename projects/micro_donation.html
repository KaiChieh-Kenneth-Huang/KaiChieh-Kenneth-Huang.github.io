Micro-donation in Grocery Stores

Donation experience design

Problem

Millennial grocery shoppers, who are interested to donate to charities, often feel pressured when asked to donate by cashiers at the checkout counters and find the information given to them about the charity insufficient.

Outcome

A portable kiosk from which customers could acquire knowledge on the charity and make donations without human pressure. The kiosk is designed to print out a slip for convenient payment of donation along with other groceries bought.
