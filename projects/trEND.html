trEND

Finding the end of a roll of sticky tape can be frustrating. And things get even worse when we fail to locate a pair of scissors nearby. This little retractable blade attached at the end of a roll of tape solves both problems.

  • System Description

System Description

The design includes two parts: 1. plastic cover 2. cutting blade with plastic slider.

  • Next

To cut the tape, the user sticks the back of the device to the tape at the desired length with the blade out and bend the tape towards the blade. Another segment can be created rapidly repeating the above procedure.

  • Next

When the blade is retracted after use, a small segment of tape naturally remains protruding from the device, making it easy to stick the device back onto the tape roll.
