Online Statistics Management System

Streamlining stats collection, management, and usage

Problem

The headquarters of the South Africa Cape Town Mission invested time weekly to collect performance statistics from missionaries via email. The stats collected were scattered in multiple files, making them difficult to analyze.

Outcome

An online automatic stats collection, processing, and visualization system built upon Google Sheets using Google Apps Script.
